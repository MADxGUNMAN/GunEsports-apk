# Uptodown Publishing Guide & Rejection Reasons

This guide explains why your APK might have been rejected by Uptodown and provides a step-by-step walkthrough for successfully publishing your app.

If your app was rejected, it likely falls into one of these categories. Check your app against this list:

### 1. Technical & Functional Issues
*   **Crashes & Bugs:** The app crashes, freezes, or doesn't work as intended during testing.
*   **Low Utility:** The app doesn't have a clear function or is just a "Hello World" test.
*   **Webviews/Templates:** Simple web wrappers (displaying a website) or unmodified app templates are often rejected.
*   **Broken Links:** If your app relies on external servers that are down or unreachable.

### 2. Content & Policy Violations
*   **Copyright Infringement:** Using assets (images, music, names) you don't own. **This is a very common reason.**
*   **Malware/Phishing:** Any sign of malicious code or data theft attempts.
*   **Impersonation:** Using icons or names that look too similar to popular existing apps (e.g., "Faceboook" or a blue 'f' icon).
*   **Inappropriate Content:** Explicit violence, hate speech, or adult content without proper flagging/rating.
*   **Scams/Spam:** Apps designed to trick users or purely for ad revenue without value.

### 3. Metadata Quality
*   **Bad Screenshots:** Blurry, stretched, or irrelevant screenshots.
*   **Poor Description:** A description that is too short, misleading, or full of keywords (keyword stuffing).
*   **Invalid Icon:** The icon is low resolution or not square.

## ðŸš€ How to Publish on Uptodown (Step-by-Step)

Follow these steps to ensure a smooth publishing process.

### Step 1: Register as a Developer
1.  Go to the [Uptodown Developers Console](https://developers.uptodown.com/).
2.  Sign up for a free account.
3.  Verify your email address.

### Step 2: Add a New App
1.  Log in to the console.
2.  Click on **"Add new app"**.
3.  Select your platform (Android).

### Step 3: Fill in App Details
*   **General Information:**
    *   **Name:** The official name of your app.
    *   **Package Name:** (e.g., `com.example.myapp`) - This is usually auto-detected from the APK.
    *   **Category:** Choose the most relevant category.
    *   **Language:** Select the primary language.

*   **Upload APK:**
    *   Upload your signed APK or AAB file.
    *   Ensure the file is not corrupted.

### Step 4: Graphic Assets (Crucial for Approval)
*   **Icon:** Upload a high-quality square icon (min 512x512 recommended).
*   **Screenshots:**
    *   Upload at least 3 screenshots.
    *   **Tip:** Use actual screenshots of the app in action, not just marketing art.
    *   Resolution matters! Avoid pixelated images.
*   **Feature Image (Optional but recommended):** A banner image for the store listing.

### Step 5: Description
*   **Short Description:** A catchy one-sentence summary.
*   **Long Description:** Explain what your app does, its features, and why users should download it. Be honest and clear.

### Step 6: Submit for Review
1.  Double-check all information.
2.  Click **"Publish"** or **"Submit"**.
3.  The Uptodown team will review your submission. This can take a few days.
